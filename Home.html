About Us

Non-profit Humanitarian Organization for Development and National Solidarity: Initiated by a group of dedicated volunteers from INPT, our organization is committed to addressing the challenges faced by Moroccan communities in need. Driven by a desire to contribute our time, energy, and knowledge, our club aims to organize visits to various orphanages, hospitals, and social centers in Rabat and its surrounding regions. Our goal is to provide underprivileged children with opportunities for sports, cultural, and religious activities, including football matches, chess games, and cultural competitions.
